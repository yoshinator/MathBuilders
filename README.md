## Why math facts matter

Kids who don't have automatic recall of basic math facts by third grade fall behind — not because they can't do math, but because their working memory is consumed by computation that should be instant. They can't focus on the actual concepts (fractions, algebra, word problems) because the basics aren't free yet.

Most "math apps" either drill kids into boredom or bury the math under so much gameplay that fluency never actually develops. MathBuilders is built for the narrow, important job in between: efficient daily practice that actually produces fluency.

## How it works (the short version)

MathBuilders uses spaced repetition — the same evidence-based approach that powers the most effective language learning and medical board prep tools — adapted specifically for math fact fluency. The system tracks each individual fact for each individual student and adjusts what they see, and how often, based on their actual recall performance.

The result is that students spend their five minutes on the facts that actually need work, not on facts they already know cold. That's why MathBuilders works in less time than worksheet-based drilling.
